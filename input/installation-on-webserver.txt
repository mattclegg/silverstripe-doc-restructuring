====== Installation on a WebServer ======

These instructions show you how to install SilverStripe on any web server.

For additional information about installing SilverStripe on specific operation systems, refer to:
  * [[http://doc.silverstripe.com/doku.php?id=installation-on-windows|Installation on a Windows Server]]
  * [[http://doc.silverstripe.com/doku.php?id=installation-on-mac-osx|Installation on OSX]]
===== Installation Steps =====
  * [[http://silverstripe.org/download|Download]] the installer package
  * Make sure the webserver has PHP 5 support, as well as one of our supported databases (MySQL (built-in), [[http://www.silverstripe.org/postgresql-module/|PostgreSQL]], [[http://www.silverstripe.org/microsoft-sql-server-database/|MSSQL]], [[http://www.silverstripe.org/sqlite-database/|SQLite3]]).  See [[server-requirements|Server Requirements]] for more information. 
  * Unpack the installer somewhere into your web-root. Usually the www folder or similar.
  * Visit your Sites Domain or IP Address in your webbrowser
  * You will be presented with a form where you enter your database login details and are asked to give your site a 'project name' and the default login details.  Fill this out and press the button.
  * After a couple of minutes, your site will be set up. Visit your Site and Enjoy!
