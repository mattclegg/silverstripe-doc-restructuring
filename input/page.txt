====== Introduction ======
The class //Page// should always be a direct subclass of [[http://api.silverstripe.org/trunk/cms/SiteTree.html|SiteTree]], and is the starting point for any customizations for your site.

**Page.php/Page.ss are NOT included in the Silverstripe-distribution, you need to create them yourself**

  * [[getting-started#the_page_type_concept]]: Understanding pagetype-inheritance
  * [[datamodel]]: Adding fields/properties to your page-type
  * [[templates]]: Creating and customizing your Page.ss base template
